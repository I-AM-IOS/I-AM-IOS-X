## Why This Dual-Licensing Model?

We built I-AM-IOS to empower individuals and the open-source community first.  
At the same time, when governments or large businesses extract significant value from this production-grade decentralized operating system, it is only fair that they contribute once — at a level proportional to the value they receive.

This model keeps the platform **free for the 95%** while ensuring sustainable development funded by the **top 5% of value holders**.
